The Story Behind C&C Care

A small business built on a simple idea — quality service should come to you, not the other way around.

Our Story

Born From Frustration.
Built With Purpose.

C&C Care started when our founder got stranded with a dead battery and was quoted $400 just to tow the car to a shop that couldn't even see them until the next day. There had to be a better way.

We built C&C Care on the belief that skilled, honest service should come to you — not the other way around. By cutting out the shop overhead, we can offer dealer-quality work at a fraction of the price, while giving you back your time.

We expanded beyond auto repair because we kept meeting the same people at broken laptops and cracked phone screens with no good options. So we added tech repair — same philosophy, same commitment, same person showing up at your door.

"We're not just fixing cars and computers. We're fixing the experience of getting things fixed."

Today C&C Care serves hundreds of clients across the area, with the same dedication to doing the job right the first time, at a fair price, with zero runaround.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every job we take, every client we serve — these are the principles we operate by.

🤝

Honesty First

We tell you what you need — not what pads our bill. No unnecessary repairs, no upselling, ever. If it doesn't need to be fixed, we'll tell you.

⚡

Respect Your Time

We show up on time, work efficiently, and communicate clearly. You shouldn't have to rearrange your life for a repair.

🎯

Done Right, First Time

We take pride in quality. If a repair isn't done right, we come back and fix it — no questions asked, at no extra charge.

💸

Fair Pricing Always

Transparent quotes before we start. No hidden fees, no surprise invoices. You know exactly what you're paying and why.

🌱

Community Focused

We're a local business serving our neighbors. We reinvest in the community and offer discounts for seniors and veterans.

🔒

Your Privacy Matters

When we work on your tech, your data is yours. We never access personal files unnecessarily and treat every device with respect.
